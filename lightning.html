Welcome to AVHomes Smart Lighting page. We specialize in providing state-of-the-art smart lighting solutions for both residential and commercial buildings. With our smart lighting, you'll never have to worry about coming home to a dark house or leaving lights on when you're not home again.

Our smart lighting allows you to control and customize the lighting in your home or office with the touch of a button, whether you're at home or away. You can easily turn lights on and off, dim them, or change the colour using your smartphone, tablet, or computer. You can also set up custom schedules to automatically turn lights on and off at specific times of the day.

Our smart lighting comes with a variety of features to fit your needs, such as voice and app control, remote access, and even integration with other smart home devices. Additionally, you can monitor and track energy usage, and set up alerts if a light is left on for an extended period.

Our team of certified professionals have the skills and experience to install and maintain your smart lighting, ensuring that it runs smoothly and efficiently. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is unmatched, and we always strive to exceed your expectations.

We are proud to be a Nigerian company and we are committed to providing our customers with the highest level of service and support. We are constantly researching new technologies to bring you the most advanced and innovative solutions for your home or office lighting.

Thank you for choosing AVHomes for your smart lighting needs. We look forward to working with you to enhance the ambience and energy efficiency of your home or office with our state-of-the-art smart lighting solutions.
